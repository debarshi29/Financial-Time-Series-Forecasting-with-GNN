gnn_node
GNNAgent runs the selected model variant on ~200–500 NIFTY stocks, outputting a score per ticker.
news_node
NewsAgent fetches headlines via yfinance and scores each with FinBERT, returning per-stock sentiment aggregates.
portfolio_node
PortfolioAgent fuses GNN rank and sentiment: final = α·GNN + (1−α)·sentiment_norm
risk_node
RiskAgent pulls live beta, 20d volatility, 52-week range, and ATR from yfinance to label stocks HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW risk.
macro_node
MarketContextAgent computes NIFTY regime (BULL / BEAR / SIDEWAYS), VIX level, and a conviction multiplier.
report_node
ReportWriterAgent uses Groq (llama3-70b) or Gemini (1.5-flash) to write a daily research note in Markdown.
